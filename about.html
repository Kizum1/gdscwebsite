GDSC at Diablo Valley College

Welcome to our student club! Our club is a community of students who are passionate about technology and development. We aim to provide a platform for students to come together, share their ideas, and collaborate on projects that align with our shared interests.

As a member of our club, you'll have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals, gain valuable skills, and explore your interests further. We offer a variety of events, workshops, and activities throughout the year to help you achieve your goals and have fun while doing it.

Our club is run by a team of dedicated students who are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. Whether you're new to the club or a returning member, we're excited to have you on board! Join us to connect with other students who share your interests and make the most of your time at DVC.
