Google Developer Student Club

GDSC at Diablo Valley College

Welcome to our student club! Google Developer Student Club is a community of students who are passionate about technology and development. We aim to provide a platform for students to come together, share their ideas, and collaborate on projects that align with our shared interests.

As a member of GDSC, you'll have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals, gain valuable skills, and explore your interests further. We offer a variety of events, workshops, and activities throughout the year to help you achieve your goals and have fun while doing it.

Our club is run by a team of dedicated students who are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. Whether you're new to the club or a returning member, we're excited to have you on board! Join us to connect with other students who share your interests and make the most of your time at DVC.

Our Mission

We aim to foster a supportive community where members can share ideas, collaborate on projects, and engage in meaningful discussions about the latest advancements in technology.

Our Values

  1. 1) Learning:
    We believe in constantly improving our skills and knowledge through hands-on experience, mentorship, and collaboration.
  2. 2) Inclusivity:
    We welcome and embrace people of all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives to create a supportive and diverse community.
  3. 3) Innovation:
    We encourage experimentation, exploration, and risk-taking to come up with new ideas and solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible.
  4. 4) Leadership:
    The club encourages members to take on leadership roles and responsibilities.
